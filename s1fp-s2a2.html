Section 1, Final Project: students will create an online publication (fan site, zine, exhibition, etc) that includes a minimum of 5 separate pages and a navigational tool/toolset. No single page and its linked content can be larger than 5 megabytes.

Section 2, Assignment 2: Students will create a self-contained, single-page site that employs hover effects and CSS selectors to assist and encourage user interaction. Your site must include a minimum of 5 images (observing optimization and best practices) and all elements must contain adequate metadata/alt text. Your site must also account for viewport sizes to serve comparable or alternate user experiences.
